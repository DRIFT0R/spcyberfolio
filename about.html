About

Background

Hi, I'm Stephano Pereira. I'm a cybersecurity student at Western Governors University working toward a career in IT and cybersecurity. I'm passionate about technology, data, and privacy. Before moving into the field, I spent years working in customer service and hospitality, which helped me develop communication, patience, a strong work ethic, and problem-solving skills that I now try to bring into technical work.

My current experience comes from certifications, coursework, homelab practice, and hands-on projects involving network security, ethical hacking, Linux and Windows systems, secure coding, risk assessment, monitoring, and technical documentation. I am looking for an entry-level role where I can keep learning, support real systems, and grow into security operations, vulnerability assessment, or infrastructure security work.

Technical Interests

I am interested in vulnerability assessment, ethical hacking, reconnaissance, OSINT, web application security, digital forensics, and defensive hardening. I like learning how systems are exposed, misconfigured, or abused, then thinking through how those risks can be reduced in a practical way to help everyday users.
